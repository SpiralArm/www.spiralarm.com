Desktop tool gives advantage

Press release, 01 November 2007

Television Research Partnership are saving time, gaining competitive advantage, and giving a better service to their customers after deploying an automation tool developed by Spiral Arm

TRP provide their clients with time-critical reports every morning, based on gathering information from a variety of sources, processing the data, and producing bespoke reports which are sent out via email. This is a process with many steps which staff performed under time pressure every day.

Spiral Arm and TRP sat down and sketched out a tool that would automate much of this work. By allowing staff to drag-and-drop components of the process into a workflow, an entire process can be automated and scheduled to run every day unattended. Should a problem be encountered, the system can retry some actions or alert TRP via SMS.

Excel, Access, FTP, email, text utilities, are among the components that can be used in the system.

A senior research manager at TRP described the system simply: "It's changed my life".
