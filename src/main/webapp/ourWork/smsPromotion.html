SMS Promotion for the rest of us.

Press release, 01 November 2009

SMS promotion was an expensive and time consuming business. Ordering a short code takes 6 to 8 weeks and then you need to pay for it for twelve months. You needed to get clearance from the regulator and finally you could get someone to integrate the service.

Now there is Taykt a new service from Spiral Arm Ltd that allows anyone to set up an SMS Promotion in less than 30 seconds. The barrier to entry for SMS Promotion is gone.*

Taykt is free for people to create SMS promotions, the customers SMSing the service pay 25 pence for the useful information.

Notes:

  • Taykt (taykt.com) is a no barrier of entry SMS Platform.
  • Spiral Arm (www.spiralarm.com) is a Brighton based software company, specializing in innovative applications.
